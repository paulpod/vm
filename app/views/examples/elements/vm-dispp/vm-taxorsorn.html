Tax or SORN the vehicle

The vehicle needs to be taxed or have a Statutory Off Road Notification made when it is purchased.

You must Tax the vehicle now (will open in a new window)

To tax, you will need to use the 12 digit document reference number from the V5C/2 (green slip) section of the registration certificate as a new keeper.

Once taxing is completed return to this page and select Continue.

If you have not already taxed or declared SORN for this vehicle, you must remind the new keeper that it's their responsibility to ensure the vehicle is taxed if it is driven on the road.

If the vehicle is being kept off the road, the new keeper must make a SORN.
