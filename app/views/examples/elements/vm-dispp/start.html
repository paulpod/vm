Selling a vehicle

Sold a vehicle out of trade

  • You’ll need the 11 digit reference number from the latest V5C vehicle registration certificate (logbook).
  • You can tax the vehicle or make a SORN (Statutory Off Road Notification) using this service.

When you can’t use this service

You can’t use this service to:

  • change a tax class of the vehicle
  • keep or assign a vehicle registration number to another vehicle
  • record joint keepers

What happens next

You need to give the green V5C/2 section to the buyer and destroy the original V5C.

The new keeper should get:

  • an email confirmation (if you provided the new keeper’s email address)
  • a new V5C registration certificate within 5 working days

Other ways to apply

You need to complete the V5C and post it to DVLA.
